Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How do I apply for a job?

+

Simply click "Apply for Jobs" on our website or message us on WhatsApp at 8390405900. Share your basic details and we'll match you with suitable positions. Our team handles everything from screening to interview coordination.

What admission programs do you support?

+

We support UG, PG, 11th-12th, diploma programs, and NEET medical counselling. Our guidance covers course selection, institute matching, documentation, and complete application submission across universities and colleges in India.

Do you assist with documentation?

+

Yes! We provide complete document packet assistance — from gathering required certificates to filling out forms and ensuring everything is ready for submission. You don't need to worry about missing any requirement.

Is there a fee for your services?

+

Initial consultation and guidance is free. Service fees, if applicable, are discussed transparently before any commitment. We believe in complete clarity — no hidden charges, no surprises.

How long does admission processing take?

+

Processing time depends on the institute and program. Typically, we complete the application process within 1-2 weeks after document collection. Our team keeps you updated at every step throughout the journey.
