FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is job placement free?

+

We offer free initial consultation. Our placement support has a nominal fee which is discussed upfront — no hidden charges at any stage.

How long does placement take?

+

Most candidates receive placement within 3–7 working days depending on the role, location, and company. We keep you updated at every step via WhatsApp.

What colleges do you support?

+

We work with universities and colleges across Maharashtra and India — covering engineering, medical, management, nursing, and more. Contact us for specific college guidance.

Do I pay before or after the website is built?

+

With our "Pay After Development" model, you only pay once the website or app is built and you're satisfied with the result. No upfront payment required.
