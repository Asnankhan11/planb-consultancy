Compliance & Transparency

Our absolute commitment to ethical and lawful academic guidance

Plan B Consultancy does not issue degrees, certificates, or academic credentials directly. All admissions and academic pathways are processed strictly through recognized institutions and are subject to university policies, eligibility criteria, and regulatory compliance.

We do not promote, guide, or support any unauthorized, fake, or illegal academic documentation.

Students are encouraged to independently verify university recognition and registration details before enrollment.
