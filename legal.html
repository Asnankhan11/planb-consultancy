If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

We are NOT an official hiring partner, authorized recruiter, representative, vendor, or authorized agency of Amazon, Flipkart, Blinkit, Myntra, Meesho, Swiggy, Zomato, or any other company unless explicitly stated in writing. You agree to provide accurate, current, and complete information during registration or application. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your communication with us and for all activities that occur under your request.

While we strive to provide the best opportunities:

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Name, Email Address, and Phone/WhatsApp Number
  • Educational Qualifications, Academic Gap years, and Resumes/CVs
  • Job Preferences, Work Experience, and Career Goals

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for:

  • Match-making with suitable Job or Admission opportunities.
  • Communicating interview schedules, document approvals, or admission updates.
  • Improving our services, platform flows, and client experience.

We do NOT sell or share your personal data to third-party marketing agencies. Your resume is shared only with potential employers or colleges relevant to your application.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

For our Women & Work From Home opportunities, candidate security is paramount. We commit to the following safety protocols:
