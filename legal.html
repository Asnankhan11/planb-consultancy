Terms of Service

1. Introduction

Welcome to Plan-B Consultancy. By accessing our website and using our services (Job Guidance, Admissions Consulting, Work From Home support), you agree to comply with these Terms of Service. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

2. Nature of Services

Plan-B Consultancy acts as a facilitator and guide. We connect students with educational institutions and job seekers with potential employers. We do not guarantee admission or employment, as final decisions rest with the respective universities and companies.

3. User Responsibilities

You agree to provide accurate, current, and complete information during registration or application. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your communication with us and for all activities that occur under your request.

4. No Guarantee Clause

While we strive to provide the best opportunities:

  • Jobs: We facilitate interviews and connections. Selection is based on your performance and the employer's requirements.
  • Admissions: We guide you through the process. Admission is subject to your eligibility and the institute's availability/norms.

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Name, Email Address, Phone Number
  • Educational Qualifications and Resumes/CVs
  • Job Preferences and Career Goals

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for:

  • Match-making with suitable Job or Admission opportunities.
  • Communicating interview schedules or admission updates.
  • improving our services and website experience.

We do NOT sell your personal data to third-party marketing agencies. Your resume is shared only with potential employers or colleges relevant to your application.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Safety & Verification Commitment

For our Women & Work From Home section, safety is paramount. We:

  • Verify the legitimacy of companies offering remote work.
  • Do not support "pay-to-work" schemes where you must pay money to get a job offer.
  • Encourage candidates to report any suspicious activity immediately.
