Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we use it.

1. Who We Are

Plan B Consultancy is an independent career support and consultancy platform based in Akola, Maharashtra, India. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or acting on behalf of any employer unless explicitly stated.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you use our services:

  • Full name, email address, and phone/WhatsApp number
  • Educational qualifications, academic history, and resumes/CVs
  • Job preferences, work experience, and career goals
  • City and location information
  • Form submission data from our job application and registration forms

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for:

  • Matching you with suitable job or admission opportunities
  • Communicating interview schedules, document approvals, or admission updates
  • Providing career guidance, interview preparation, and resume assistance
  • Improving our services and client experience
  • Sending relevant career opportunity updates (with your consent)

We do NOT sell or share your personal data to third-party marketing agencies. Your resume is shared only with potential employers relevant to your application, and only with your knowledge.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to contact us immediately if you suspect any misuse of your data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at planb.consultancy.in@gmail.com.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., Google Forms, WhatsApp, social media). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these websites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
